Crafting Gameplays Redstone Building and Designs


Redstone is an ingredient/item that can be obtained in minecraft through redstone ore, killing witches, trading with villages and in temples.

Redstone is widely used in minecraft to create mechanical systems.


Learning redstone is important for children as it allows children to learn about circuits which is an important thing for children to learn in this day and age.

Redstone itself can be used in a multitude of ways, it forms the spine of all minecraft circuits.

Using redstone can allow you to make features such as dispensers, droppers, redstone torches, redstone repeaters, pistons and redstone circuit lines.


In this page you will find redstone guides in tutorial to assist you in your minecraft journey.
